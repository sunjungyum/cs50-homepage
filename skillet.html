Sweet Potato 'n Chicken Sausage Skillet

Ingredients

  • 1 small sweet potato, chopped
  • 4-3 baby bella mushrooms, sliced
  • 1 Italian chicken sausage, sliced
  • 1/2 cup chickpeas, rinsed and drained
  • 3-4 handfuls arugula
  • 1/2 avocado, diced
  • 1 egg
  • 2-4 tsp olive oil
  • 1-2 tbsp pine nuts
  • Pink Himalayan sea salt
  • Cracked black pepper
  • Chili pepper flakes

Directions

In a skillet (I use cast iron), sauté the sweet potato, mushrooms, chicken sausage in 1-2 tsp of olive oil. When cooked through, add a bit of 2-3 handfuls of arugula and the chickpeas. Season to taste.

In a separate skillet, fry the egg in another 1-2 tsp of olive oil. Once it’s cooked, add it on top of the vegetables, chickpeas, and sausage, along with the avocado. Top with pine nuts, black pepper, and chili pepper flakes!

And, the best part: crack the yolk!
