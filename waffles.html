Chocolate & Raspberry Waffles

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Arrowhead Mills Buckwheat Waffle Mix
  • 3 scoops Terra Origin Mint Healthy Gut Powder
  • 1 egg
  • 3/4 cup water
  • 1 tbsp pure maple syrup
  • 1 tbsp coconut oil, melted
  • 2 cups fresh or frozen raspberries
  • 3/4 cup Greek or Icelandic yogurt
  • 3 blocks of dark chocolate
  • 1/3 cup sliced almonds

Directions

In a medium bowl, prepare your waffle mix by beating together the egg, water, coconut oil, and maple syrup. Add in the waffle mix and Healthy Gut Powder. Let sit in the fridge if possible.

Grease your waffle iron lightly, and cook your waffles to your liking. I like making them fairly small, which is why it makes 4-6 per serving. If you make them bigger, you will probably get 2.
