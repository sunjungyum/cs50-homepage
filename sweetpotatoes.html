Roasted Rosemary Sweet Potatoes

Ingredients

  • 1.5 lb sweet potatoes
  • 2 tbsp rosemary leaves
  • 1-2 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  • 1-3 garlic cloves
  • 1/2-1 tsp pink Himalayan sea salt
  • 1/2 tsp cracked black pepper

Directions

Line a large baking sheet with parchment paper and pre-heat your oven to 400 degrees F.

Meanwhile, wash the sweet potatoes (and peel, if desired). Mince 1â€“3 garlic cloves, based on how much you enjoy garlic. Toss together the sweet potatoes, rosemary, olive oil, garlic, sea salt, and pepper.

Spread the sweet potatoes on the lined baking sheet and bake in the oven for around 40-50 minutes, tossing occasionally. Take out and serve!!
